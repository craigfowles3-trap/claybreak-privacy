Delete your ClayFowl account & data

This page explains how to delete your ClayFowl account and the data associated with it. ClayFowl is published by ClayFowl.

Option 1 — Delete it yourself, in the app

  1. Open the ClayFowl app.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all of your cloud-synced data, then signs you out.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email us from the address on your account:

hello@clayfowl.app — subject line Delete my account.

We will delete your account and all associated cloud data within 30 days of your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login email address
  • All cloud-synced data: your sessions, rounds, scores, notes, and equipment setups

What is kept

Nothing is retained after your account is deleted. Any cloud backups are purged within 30 days. ClayFowl is local-first — if you never signed in, none of your data ever left your device, and data stored only on your device is removed when you delete your account in the app or uninstall it.
