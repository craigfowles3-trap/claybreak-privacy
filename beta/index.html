Help test ClayFowl

ClayFowl is a shooting journal for trap, skeet, and sporting clays — and it needs beta testers before it can launch. Even just installing it helps. Takes about two minutes.

1

Join the tester group

Google only lets group members install the beta. One tap, using the Google account already on your phone.

Join the tester group Opens in a new tab — come back here for step 2.
2

Opt in as a tester

Sign in with that same Google account, then tap the blue Become a tester button.

Opt in as a tester Opens in a new tab — come back here for step 3.
3

Install ClayFowl

Tap Install on Google Play. Seeing “Something went wrong on our end”, stuck on Pending, or told it isn't available yet? All normal — tester access takes ~15 minutes to sync. Try again shortly.

Open in Google Play
🎉 You're all set — you're officially a tester. Thank you!
The single biggest way you help: keep it installed for at least 2 weeks. Google requires testers to stay enrolled for 14 straight days before the app can launch publicly. You don't have to use it every day — though if you shoot, logging a real round is gold.
