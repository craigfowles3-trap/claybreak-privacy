Help test ClayFowl

ClayFowl is a shooting journal for trap, skeet, and sporting clays — and it needs beta testers before it can launch. Even just installing it helps. Takes about two minutes.

1

Join the tester group

Google only lets approved testers install the beta — joining this group approves you. One tap, uses the account already on your phone.

2

Become a tester

Now that you're approved, accept the invite.

Become a tester
3

Install ClayFowl

Install it from Google Play like any other app.

Install from Google Play
The single biggest way you help: keep it installed for at least 2 weeks. Google requires testers to stay enrolled for 14 straight days before the app can launch publicly. You don't have to use it every day — though if you shoot, logging a real round is gold.

Get TestFlight
