ClayFowl

Your clay-target shooting journal.

Coming soon to iOS & Android

ClayFowl is a simple, private logbook for the clay sports. Track every round you shoot, remember what gun and load you ran, and start seeing what actually moves your scores — without ads, accounts, or anyone selling your data.

Built for trap · skeet · sporting clays

What it does

📝

Log every round

Record sessions and rounds — targets, hits, yardage, and scores — in seconds at the line.

🔫

Track your setup

Save your gun, choke, and shell combinations and tie them to the rounds you shoot.

📍

Range & weather

Auto-identify the range you're at and capture the conditions for each session.

📈

See what's working

Spot trends across disciplines, equipment, and conditions over time.

Going deeper · free while in beta

ClayFowl is free and complete on its own. ClayFowl Pro takes your range journal further — on the web.

📊

Web analytics dashboard

Open your journal on the web and dig into long-term trends, gear-vs-conditions comparisons, and your weak stations — all on a bigger screen.

☁️

Sync across devices

An optional cloud backup keeps your journal in step on every device you shoot with.

Get ClayFowl Pro

Free while in beta — sign in with the same account you use in the ClayFowl app.

Your data stays yours

Privacy isn't a setting in ClayFowl — it's the default.

📴

Offline, no account needed

Everything works without signing in. If you never sign in, your journal never leaves your phone.

🚫

No ads, no trackers

No advertising, no third-party analytics, no tracking SDKs. None.

🛡️

Never sold

We don't sell your data — and we never will.

🤏

Only the minimum

Just your journal — and only if you opt in, your email and an approximate range location, used once per session and never tracked in the background.

📤

Export or delete, anytime

Take everything with you as CSV, or delete your account and all cloud data — on your terms.

🔐

Sync is yours alone

Optional cloud sync is locked to your account — only you can read it.
