Claybreak

Your clay-target shooting journal.

📱 Coming soon to iOS & Android

Claybreak is a simple, private logbook for the clay sports. Track every round you shoot, remember what gun and load you ran, and start seeing what actually moves your scores — without ads, accounts, or anyone selling your data.

Built for trap · skeet · sporting clays

What it does

📝

Log every round

Record sessions and rounds — targets, hits, yardage, and scores — in seconds at the line.

🔫

Track your setup

Save your gun, choke, and shell combinations and tie them to the rounds you shoot.

📍

Range & weather

Auto-identify the range you're at and capture the conditions for each session.

📈

See what's working

Spot trends across disciplines, equipment, and conditions over time.

🔒

Private by default

Works fully offline with no account. No ads, no trackers, no selling your data.

☁️

Optional sync

Sign in only if you want a cloud backup and to sync your journal across devices.
